Sahayog is not-for-profit interface working for betterment of people. We mainly focus is to look after the physical and mental health of elders, childrens and senior citizens and make them interact more with younger generations without regard to age, race, religion, or ability to pay. Sahayog try best to empower youths through relevant education, innovative healthcare, and livelihood programs. Sahayog provide a homely and respectable living for the Elders with utmost commitment, dedication, trust and care to promote compassion and love. Sahyog is committed to helping people create healthy atmosphere where the mental health of citizens is valued and protected. We always dream of a society based on legitimate rights, equity, justice, honesty, social sensitivity, and a culture of service in which all are self-reliant. Our main goal is the empowerment of women and girls for the betterment and takes out them from poverty, discrimination, and violations.
